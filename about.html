Keenan Daniels is a Stellenbosch-based artist renowned for his photorealistic pen and pencil sketches rendered in striking black and white. His work captures vintage portraits, such as iconic figures like Marilyn Monroe, alongside detailed depictions of animals, architecture, and various photographic subjects, all executed with near pixel-perfect precision. Primarily working with pencil and charcoal, Keenan also experiments by blending multiple mediums within a single piece, pushing the boundaries of traditional sketching to create uniquely textured artworks.
